Pasta

A simple italian inspired meal made with pasta chicken and a rich sauce

Preparation Time

  • Preparation time : 10min
  • Cooking time : 20min
  • Total time : 30min

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Chicken breast
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Tomato

Instructions

  1. Boil the pasta
  2. cook the chicken
  3. prepare the sauce
  4. Mixed the pasta with the sauce
  5. add cheese
  6. serve it
